WHAT IS SUB2UNLOCK

The platform is used by some Minecraft creators to grow their audience. Sub2Unlock is also called sub2unlock.com or sub to unlock. The goal is to complete the tasks, unlock the link, and reach the final file host.

HOW TO USE SUB2UNLOCK

2. The page will show a list of tasks, usually 2 to 4. Do not click any banner ads. Only interact with the task list.
3. For each task, use the smart skip method first. Click the task to open it. Immediately go back to the Sub2Unlock page. The task often registers as completed without you needing to actually subscribe or follow.
4. If the smart skip does not work, perform the actual task. Subscribe to the YouTube channel, follow the Twitter account, or join the Discord server. Then go back to the Sub2Unlock page.
5. When all tasks show green checkmarks, the Unlock or Continue button will become clickable. Scroll down if you do not see it. Some tasks may be hidden lower on the page.
6. Click the Unlock button. You will be redirected to the real file host like MediaFire or Google Drive. Download your file from there.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: Progress keeps resetting or tasks do not save.
SOLUTION: Switch to Chrome or Brave browser. In app browsers from Discord and YouTube break session tracking.

PROBLEM: Tasks do not register as completed.
SOLUTION: Open the task and wait 10 seconds before going back. If it still fails, do the actual subscribe or follow action.

PROBLEM: The Unlock button stays grey even after all tasks are done.
SOLUTION: Scroll down. Look for hidden tasks below the visible area. Re open each task and return to refresh the progress.

PROBLEM: Pop ups and new tabs keep opening.
SOLUTION: Close them immediately. They are advertisements, not part of the tasks. Use Brave browser to block many of them.

PROBLEM: I am asked to install software or allow notifications.
SOLUTION: Never install anything. Never allow notifications. These are scam ads. Close the tab and go back to the main page.
