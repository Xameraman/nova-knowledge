WHAT IS MODRINTH

Modrinth is an open source Minecraft modding platform founded in 2020. It is based in Tempe Arizona USA. Modrinth hosts mods, plugins, data packs, shaders, resource packs, and modpacks for Java Edition only. Modrinth supports Forge, Fabric, Quilt, and NeoForge mod loaders. The platform is fully open source under the GNU AGPLv3 license. Modrinth has a website at modrinth.com and a free desktop app called the Modrinth App. Modrinth has over twelve thousand creators and millions of players. Creators earn money through ad revenue sharing where 75 percent goes to creators. Modrinth also offers a Modrinth Plus subscription for ad free browsing.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM MODRINTH

1. Go to modrinth.com and search for the mod, modpack, or resource pack you want. Use the search bar or browse by category.
2. Click the project you want from the search results. The project page shows the description, images, and version information. Check the project name and icon match what you need.
3. Before downloading, check compatibility. Look at three things: the Minecraft version must match your game, the mod loader must match what you have installed either Forge, Fabric, Quilt, or NeoForge, and the environment must say Client or Both for most players.
4. Click the Versions tab on the project page. This shows all available files with their supported Minecraft version and mod loader. Modrinth may highlight a version for you but always double check manually.
5. Click the Download button next to the correct version. The file saves as a .jar for mods or .zip for resource packs. Modrinth has no fake download buttons so the button you click is the real one.
6. Open your Downloads folder and find the file. Do not double click .jar files. You only need to place them in the correct folder.
7. Open your .minecraft folder. On Windows press Win plus R, type percent appdata percent, and open .minecraft. On macOS use Cmd plus Shift plus G and paste the path to Application Support minecraft. On Linux go to tilde .minecraft.
8. Inside .minecraft create a folder named mods in lowercase if it does not exist. Drag the .jar file into the mods folder.
9. Launch the Minecraft Launcher and select the correct profile for your mod loader, such as Fabric Loader or Forge. Press Play and the mod should load. Check the Mods menu to confirm.
10. For the easiest method, download the Modrinth App from modrinth.com/app. Sign in with your Microsoft account. Browse mods and click Install. The app handles all dependencies, versions, and mod loaders automatically.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: Mod does not work after installation.
SOLUTION: Check the mod loader matches. A Fabric mod will not work with Forge. Check the Minecraft version matches exactly. Look for missing dependencies like Fabric API or Cloth Config on the mod page.

PROBLEM: Game crashes on launch after installing a mod.
SOLUTION: Read the crash report. It usually names the problematic mod. Remove recently added mods one by one to find the cause. Check all mods are for the correct Minecraft version.

PROBLEM: Modrinth App cannot connect or install modpacks.
SOLUTION: Check your internet connection. Try restarting the app. If the problem continues, reinstall the Modrinth App from the official website.

PROBLEM: Search on the Modrinth website is not finding what I need.
SOLUTION: Try different keywords. Use the filters on the left side to narrow by mod loader, version, and category. Refresh the page or clear your browser cache.

PROBLEM: Downloaded file is .zip but I expected .jar.
SOLUTION: Some content like resource packs and data packs come as .zip files. Place .zip resource packs in the resourcepacks folder. Place .zip data packs in the datapacks folder inside your world save.

PROBLEM: I do not know which mod loader I have installed.
SOLUTION: Open your Minecraft Launcher and look at the profile name. It will say Forge, Fabric, Quilt, or NeoForge if you have a mod loader installed.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

· Modrinth is for Java Edition only. There is no Bedrock Edition support on Modrinth.
· Never double click .jar files. Just place them in the mods folder.
· Always check the Relations tab on the mod page for required dependencies like Fabric API.
· Do not download mods from unofficial or repackaged sources. Only use modrinth.com.
· Modrinth has clean ads under the Acceptable Ads Standard. No fake download buttons exist.
· The Modrinth App is free and open source. Never pay for a mod installer claiming to be Modrinth.
