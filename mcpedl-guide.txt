WHAT IS MCPEDL

MCPEDL stands for Minecraft PE Download and is the largest website for Bedrock Edition content. Founded in 2013, MCPEDL hosts over ten thousand projects including addons, maps, texture packs, skin packs, mods, seeds, and scripts. MCPEDL is completely free and covers all Bedrock platforms: mobile, Windows 10/11, Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch. MCPEDL is also spelled mcpedl.com or MCPE DL. MCPEDL does not require an account to download. All MCPEDL content is community uploaded and free to access.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM MCPEDL

1. Go to mcpedl.com in your browser. Use the search bar at the top or browse the categories like Addons, Maps, or Texture Packs.
2. Find the content you want from the search results. Click the title or thumbnail to open the full content page for that project.
3. On the content page look for a Skip to Download button or link at the very top of the page. Click it to jump directly to the download section. If there is no Skip to Download button, scroll all the way to the bottom of the page. The download links are always at the bottom.
4. In the download section you will see one or more download links labelled by Minecraft version, such as v1.20 or v1.21. Choose the version that matches your game version. To check your Minecraft version, open Minecraft, go to Settings, then About.
5. Click the real download link. The real link is small green text inside the content area, NOT a large flashy banner ad. It is usually labelled with the version number. Avoid any large DOWNLOAD banners or animated buttons. Those are advertisements.
6. The download link may go directly to a file like .mcpack or .mcaddon, or it may lead to an external host like MediaFire, Google Drive, or Dropbox. If it goes through Linkvertise or another ad wall, complete the required tasks and wait for the final redirect.
7. Once the file downloads, open your file manager. On Android use ZArchiver for best results. On iOS use the Files app. Go to your Downloads folder and locate the downloaded file.
8. If the file is .mcpack, .mcaddon, or .mcworld, simply tap it and choose Minecraft to open it. Minecraft will display Import Started followed by Successfully Imported. If the file is .zip, rename it first: remove .zip and make the extension .mcpack or .mcaddon, then tap it and choose Minecraft.
9. After importing, activate the pack. For resource packs go to Settings, Global Resources, and activate the pack. For behaviour packs in a world, edit the world, go to Behaviour Packs, and activate the pack. For .mcaddon files, activate both the Resource Pack and the Behaviour Pack separately.
10. Some addons require Experimental Gameplay features. Check the MCPEDL page description for which experiments to enable. Go to World Settings, Game, Experiments and turn on the required toggles.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: I clicked a big DOWNLOAD button but nothing downloads.
SOLUTION: That button was an advertisement. Go back to the MCPEDL page and find the small green text link inside the content area, below the changelog or description. That is the real download link.

PROBLEM: My file downloaded as .zip instead of .mcpack.
SOLUTION: Rename the file extension. Remove .zip and type .mcpack or .mcaddon. Then tap the file and choose Minecraft to import. Also check the MCPEDL page to confirm the expected file type.

PROBLEM: Import failed or the file does not open.
SOLUTION: Try using "Open with" and select Minecraft. Make sure your file extension is .mcpack, .mcaddon, or .mcworld. If it is .zip, rename it first. Make sure Minecraft is installed and up to date. On Android, check that Minecraft has storage permission in your device settings.

PROBLEM: I imported the addon but it does not appear in my world.
SOLUTION: For .mcaddon files, activate both the Behaviour Pack and the Resource Pack in your world settings. If the addon needs Experimental Gameplay, enable the required toggles in World Settings. Also check that the addon is compatible with your Minecraft version.

PROBLEM: The download link is broken or expired.
SOLUTION: Contact the creator using their social links on the MCPEDL page. Ask in the MIA Discord server for an updated link. Report the broken link in the MCPEDL comments section so the creator knows.

PROBLEM: I see a Linkvertise page after clicking the MCPEDL download link.
SOLUTION: This is the creator's choice to monetize their content. Complete the free tasks on the Linkvertise page to unlock the real download. Use the MIA Linkvertise guide for step by step help completing those tasks.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

· Never click large flashy DOWNLOAD banners on MCPEDL. They are advertisements.
· The real download link is always small green text inside the content area of the page.
· Do not download .jar files from MCPEDL. They are for Java Edition and will not work on Bedrock.
· MCPEDL files usually go to MediaFire, Google Drive, or Dropbox. Expect to see an external host.
· Some MCPEDL creators use Linkvertise or Sub2Unlock. The MIA guides explain how to handle those.
· Console users cannot directly install MCPEDL files. Download on a phone or PC first, then sync via a Microsoft account.
