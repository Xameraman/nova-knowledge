WHAT IS PROTON DRIVE

Proton Drive is an encrypted cloud storage service from Proton AG based in Switzerland. Launched in 2022 it offers end to end encryption for files file names and metadata. Proton Drive has no ads no fake download buttons and no transfer quotas. Free accounts give 5 GB of storage. Proton Drive website is at drive.proton.me. Proton Drive is also called proton drive or simply proton. Many Minecraft creators use Proton Drive for clean ad free sharing.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD FROM PROTON DRIVE

1. Open the Proton Drive link. The URL contains drive.proton.me.
2. If a password prompt appears enter the password given by the creator. Then submit.
3. You will see a clean page with the file name file size and a Download button. There are no ads.
4. Click the Download button. Your browser will decrypt the file before saving. This may take a few seconds to several minutes depending on file size and your device speed.
5. After decryption completes your browser will start the download or ask where to save. Choose your Downloads folder.
6. Locate the downloaded file. For Minecraft if it is .mcpack or .mcaddon tap to import. If it is .jar place in mods folder. If .mcworld tap to import the world.
7. If the download seems stuck wait longer. Proton Drive is decrypting in your browser. This is a privacy feature not a bug.

COMMON PROBLEMS

PROBLEM: Download does not start after clicking Download.
SOLUTION: Wait. Your browser is decrypting the file. This can take time on large files. If over 5 minutes refresh the page once.

PROBLEM: Download speed is slow.
SOLUTION: Proton Drive does not limit speeds. Check your internet connection. Use Wi Fi if on mobile.

PROBLEM: Access Denied or Link Invalid error appears.
SOLUTION: The share link may have expired or been revoked. Ask the creator for a new link.

PROBLEM: Password prompt appears but I have no password.
SOLUTION: Contact the person who shared the link to get the password. Proton Drive passwords are set by the file owner.

PROBLEM: Download fails on iPhone or iPad.
SOLUTION: Safari may struggle with large files. Use Chrome for iOS or the free Proton Drive app from the App Store.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

路 Proton Drive has no ads. Any pop up asking for money is a scam.
路 Never download third party tools that claim to speed up Proton Drive downloads. They are malware.
路 The decryption delay is normal. Do not close the page thinking it is broken.
路 Use a modern browser like Chrome Firefox Edge or Brave. JavaScript must be enabled.
路 On iOS use the Proton Drive app for files larger than 100 MB to avoid browser limits.
